The creature inside of me

The creature is inside is lurking

It is ready to bite

to destroy the work I have done

But yet if I ignore it

it will only bite harder

 

Everyday I feel it

The sharp spikes poking me

The sliminess evading what I do to kill it

 

I cannot seem to do away with it

it is parasite

yet it is me

And it seems it is all that I am

 

I was once told

“the artic winter is 6 months long”

“during the artic winter there is only darkness”

No light

And no hope
